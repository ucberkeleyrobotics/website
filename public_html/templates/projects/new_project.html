Starting a New Project

Want to run a project of your own? Need money to purchase materials? Submit a project proposal and receive Robotics Team support!

To submit a proposal for a new project, fill out our project proposal form. After you do this, you will be contacted by a leader of Robotics@Berkeley with a request for more specific information, such as what parts you need and what the deadlines will be for your project. If your proposal is approved for Robotics@Berkeley support, you will be assigned a R@B advisor who will work to get your project all the support it needs to be sucessful.
