Join

Why Join?

Robotics at Berkeley gives you the chance to put your awesome robotics ideas to the test. Whether you want to build a combat robot, work on a self-driving vehicle, or program quadcopters to dance to music, we can help you get the funding, connections, and information you need to actually build the robot of your dreams. Furthermore, by joining the robotics team, you'll be surrounding yourself by other self-motivated and hands-on roboticists at Cal. And even futhermore, by joining this club you'll have increased access to robotics companies and labs - connections that will certainly help you in the future if you pursue a career in robotics.

Are there any requirements to join?

The only requirement is that you're passionate about working on an awesome robotics project! This being our first year, we expect members to have high initiative and adaptability. If you're willing to put in the effort, you'll certainly be able to contribute significantly.

How to Join:

Please email ucbrobotics@gmail.com to be added to our mailing list. All club meetings and updates will be sent through the mailing list.
