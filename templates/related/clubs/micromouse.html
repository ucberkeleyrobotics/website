IEEE Micromouse


Website: ieee.berkeley.edu

Contact: ieee [at] berkeley.edu

Description:

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is an international organization that aims to further awareness, growth, and organization of activities in the electrical engineering and computer science community.

In their annual Micromouse competition, students collaborate to create and build a mouse that autonomously navigates through a maze. The mouse must be self-contained, battery-operated, and discover routes with brief run times. Micromouse must also avoid collisions with maze walls.
