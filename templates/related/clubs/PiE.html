Pioneers in Engineering (PiE)


Website: pioneers.berkeley.edu

Contact:leadership [at] pioneers.berkeley.edu

Description:

Pioneers in Engineering (PiE) is a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) outreach student group at UC Berkeley. Their goal is to inspire under-served high school students in the Bay Area to pursue engineering and higher education, by engaging them in robotics and other hands-on engineering projects.

PiE Robotics Competition - Pioneers in Engineering organizes an annual 8-week robotics competition during the Spring that provides high school students with an immersive engineering challenge. To host the competition, PiE members design the games, provide mentorship, and develop the robotics kit that are used by the +350 high school students. The competition costs each team only $100, which is significantly more affordable and accessible than comparable robotics competitions.
