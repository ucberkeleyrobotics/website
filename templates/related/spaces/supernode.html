Supernode


Website:supernode.berkeley.edu

Location: 246 Cory Hall

Hours: 24/7 schedule

Contact: supernode[at]lists.berkeley.edu

Directions:

Enter Cory from Hearst Avenue and Supernode can be found in the center of the second floor.

Descriptions:

Located in Cory Hall, Supernode is a student-run makerspace with resources available to students from generous sponsors. Check out their introductory video here.

The space is equipped with a wide variety of tools and materials such as 3D printers, solder and soldering irons, oscilloscopes, multimeters, logic analyzers, development boards, and parts that are great for robotics or hardware projects.

How to Gain Access:

Supernode is open as long as a member with card access is present. To receive card access to the facility, sign up for a training session here. Although there is no membership fee, Supernode certainly welcomes donations to restock supplies for the space.
