Sponsorships

Sponsors of Robotics@Berkeley enjoy numerous benefits for helping our organization. Sponsoring our project teams and events will give your firm name-brand recognition across campus. The branding on our t-shirts and websites will also give your company recognition off-campus when we go to competitions and events in our community and around the country. Depending on how much your company donates, Robotics@Berkeley will also organize an on-campus info-session for your organization, giving your firm even more exposure to roboticists here at Berkeley. For more specific details about sponsorship, please take a look at the sponsorship packet here for more detailed information.
